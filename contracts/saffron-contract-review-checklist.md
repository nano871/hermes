# SAFFRON — CONTRACT REVIEW CHECKLIST

**For:** Incoming contracts from clients, vendors, partners, or platforms
**How to use:** Scan each section. Red flag = stop and consult Counsel Prime before signing.

---

## PHASE 1: PRE-REVIEW (2 minutes)

| Check | What to Look For | Red Flag |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| **☐ Contract type** | Is this an MSA, SOW, NDA, SaaS ToS, or clickwrap? | You're not sure what you're signing |
| **☐ Your entity** | Is Saffron Automations LLC named correctly? | Wrong entity name or misspelled |
| **☐ Counterparty** | Who are you contracting with? Is it the right legal entity? | Just a DBA or person's name |
| **☐ Signatures** | Does it require your signature? Where? | You're expected to sign without reading |
| **☐ Governing law** | What state's law applies? | Not Minnesota |

## PHASE 2: LIABILITY (5 minutes)

| Clause | IDEAL for Saffron | Yellow Flag | 🔴 Red Flag |
|--------|------------------|-------------|------------|
| **Liability Cap** | 1x fees paid (12 months) | 2x-3x fees | No cap, or cap below fees paid |
| **Consequential Damages** | Mutual waiver with standard carve-outs | Waiver without carve-outs | No waiver, or one-sided carve-outs |
| **Indemnity — You indemnify them** | Only for IP infringement of your work product | + gross negligence | Broad: "any claim arising from your services" |
| **Indemnity — They indemnify you** | For their IP, misuse of deliverables, data | Mutual IP only | No indemnity from them |
| **Insurance required** | GL $1M + E&O $1M | GL only | E&O excluded or amounts you can't get |

## PHASE 3: SCOPE & PAYMENT (3 minutes)

| Clause | IDEAL | Yellow Flag | 🔴 Red Flag |
|--------|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Scope definition** | Clear, specific, measurable | Vague or open-ended | "and other services as needed" |
| **Change order process** | Written CO required for changes | Email approval OK | Verbal changes permitted |
| **Payment terms** | Net 30 | Net 45 or 60 | Net 60+ or milestone-based without protection |
| **Late payment** | 1.5%/mo interest + suspension right | Interest only | No late payment penalty |
| **Kill fee** | 100% accrued + 50% remaining | 100% accrued only | No kill fee — cancel anytime with only accrued paid |
| **Expenses** | Pre-approved, reimbursed at cost | Reimbursed but no pre-approval | You pay all expenses from fees |

## PHASE 4: IP (3 minutes)

| Clause | IDEAL | Yellow Flag | 🔴 Red Flag |
|--------|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Work product ownership** | Client owns deliverables you create | Joint ownership | "Works made for hire" covering all your work |
| **Pre-existing IP** | Yours stays yours | License to client | Assigned to client |
| **AI-generated content** | Disclaimed (not warrantied) | Not mentioned | Warranted as non-infringing/copyrightable |
| **Residual knowledge** | You keep generalized skills/methods | Not mentioned | You can't use skills learned on this project |
| **License-back to you** | You can use generalized knowledge | Limited to internal use | None — everything is client's |

## PHASE 5: TERMINATION & DISPUTES (2 minutes)

| Clause | IDEAL | Yellow Flag | 🔴 Red Flag |
|--------|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Term for convenience** | Mutual, 30 days | Client-only termination right | You can't terminate without cause |
| **Term for cause** | 15-30 day cure period | Immediate termination for any breach | No cure period — any breach = termination |
| **Survival** | IP, confidentiality, liability survive | Only IP survives | Nothing survives |
| **Dispute resolution** | MN law, MN venue | Arbitration in MN | Non-MN venue or law |

## PHASE 6: SPECIAL CLAUSES (3 minutes)

| Clause | What to Check |
|--------|---------------|
| **☐ Most favored nation** | If you give another client better terms, do you have to give them to this client? |
| **☐ Non-solicitation** | Can they hire your people? Can you hire theirs? |
| **☐ Publicity rights** | Can they use your name/logo in case studies? Requires approval. |
| **☐ Audit rights** | Can they audit your books/security? Limit to once/year with notice. |
| **☐ Data processing** | If you handle their data, do you need a DPA? |
| **☐ SLA / uptime** | If this is a SaaS contract, what uptime is guaranteed? What are the credits? |
| **☐ Auto-renewal** | Does it auto-renew? How do you cancel? |
| **☐ Assignment** | Can they assign without your consent? (Should require consent) |

## QUICK DECISION GUIDE

| Assessment | Action |
|------------|--------|
| **🟢 CLEAR** — All clauses in IDEAL or ACCEPTABLE range | Sign |
| **🟡 DEFENSIBLE** — 1-2 yellow flags, manageable risk | Sign with notes for next time |
| **🟠 NEGOTIATE** — 1 red flag or 3+ yellow flags | Counter required |
| **🔴 STOP** — Multiple red flags or terms you cannot accept | Do not sign. Come back to Counsel Prime. |

---

## KEY NEGOTIATION PRINCIPLES

1. **Liability cap is the hardest battle — win it first.** Everything else is easier after the cap is set.
2. **Never agree to "works made for hire"** — it captures pre-existing IP. Use "assignment of deliverables specifically created for this engagement."
3. **AI disclaimers are non-negotiable** — Saffron cannot warrant AI output copyrightability, accuracy, or non-infringement. If the client insists, push to a separate SOW with adjusted pricing.
4. **Kill fee protects your pipeline** — without it, a client can cancel after you've staffed up and you eat the cost. Never waive it.
5. **No non-MN venue** — litigating in California, New York, or Delaware costs 5-10x more than MN. Stand firm on this.
6. **If they push for higher liability caps:** Offer tiered pricing — higher cap = higher rate. The cap is a business term, not a legal term.
