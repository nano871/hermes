**Version:** 1.0

---

This Acceptable Use Policy (**"AUP"**) governs your use of the Saffron Dashboard platform (the **"Platform"**). It is incorporated by reference into the Terms of Service. Capitalized terms not defined here have the meaning given in the Terms of Service.

**Violation of this AUP may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account.**

---

## 1. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

You shall not, and shall not permit others to, use the Platform to:

### 1.1 Illegal Activity
Engage in or promote any activity that violates applicable law, regulation, or government order, including but not limited to:
- Fraud, money laundering, or unlawful gambling
- Copyright, trademark, or patent infringement
- Export control violations
- Data privacy violations

### 1.2 Harmful Content
Upload, store, share, or generate:
- Malware, viruses, ransomware, or other malicious code
- Content that is defamatory, harassing, threatening, or discriminatory
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — **zero tolerance: immediate reporting to NCMEC**
- Content that incites violence or terrorism
- Deepfakes or manipulated media intended to deceive or harm

### 1.3 Unauthorized Access
- Attempt to bypass authentication, security controls, or access restrictions
- Attempt to access another user's account or data without authorization
- Probe, scan, or test the Platform's vulnerability without written authorization
- Use any robot, spider, scraper, or automated tool to access the Platform without our written permission (except for our public API per its terms)

### 1.4 Abuse of AI Features
- Use AI-generated outputs in regulated decisions (hiring, credit, housing, insurance, healthcare) without independent human review and compliance validation
- Use the Platform to generate deceptive or fraudulent content (fake reviews, impersonation, disinformation)
- Attempt to extract, reverse engineer, or replicate the Platform's AI models or training data
- Submit content to the Platform that is designed to "jailbreak," prompt-inject, or manipulate AI features beyond their intended capabilities

### 1.5 Resource Abuse
- Exceed documented usage limits (API rate limits, storage quotas, concurrent user limits)
- Use the Platform in a way that degrades performance for other users
- Attempt to circumvent usage limits through the creation of multiple accounts

### 1.6 Commercial Restrictions
- Resell, sublicense, or timeshare access to the Platform
- Use the Platform to build a competitive product or service
