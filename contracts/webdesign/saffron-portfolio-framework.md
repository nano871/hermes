SAFFRON DESIGNS — PORTFOLIO PERMISSION FRAMEWORK

Purpose: Track which client projects you can display in your portfolio,
and under what terms. Prevents "you showed my site without permission" disputes.

PERMISSION LEVELS

Level 1 — Full Portfolio Rights (Standard)
• Display screenshots, case study, live URL
• Use in portfolio, social media, marketing materials
• Attribution to Saffron Designs
• Source: Written in Web Design Agreement §3.6

Level 2 — Limited Display
• Display screenshots only (no case study, no live URL)
• No attribution required
• Source: Client requested confidentiality for business details

Level 3 — No Display
• Cannot display in portfolio
• Source: Client confidentiality agreement, NDA, or trade secret concerns
• May still use as internal reference

Level 4 — Credit Link Only
• Footer credit link ("Website by Saffron Designs") is sufficient
• No active portfolio display needed
• Source: Web Design Agreement §3.7 or separate agreement

CONFIDENTIALITY CHECKLIST (before posting)

Before publishing any project in your portfolio:
☐ Permission level confirmed (from agreement or separate approval)
☐ No confidential information exposed (trade secrets, financial data, proprietary processes)
☐ Client's business information is accurate and up to date
☐ Screenshots reflect the final live site (not early mockups)
☐ Client has been notified (optional but professional)
☐ Any NDA restrictions reviewed and accommodated

WHAT NOT TO SHOW
• Internal admin panels or CMS views
• Login credentials or access URLs
• Client analytics data, revenue figures, or conversion rates
• Content that client has not yet published
• Projects where client explicitly declined portfolio rights

WITHDRAWING PERMISSION
If a client later requests removal from portfolio:
• Remove within [5] business days
• Send confirmation of removal
