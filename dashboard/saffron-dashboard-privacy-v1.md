## 1. SCOPE

This Privacy Policy describes how Saffron Automations LLC (**"Saffron," "we," "us,"** or **"our"**) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information about users of the Saffron Dashboard platform (the **"Platform"**). This policy applies to all users of the Platform, including account holders, visitors, and API users.

This policy does not apply to information we process on behalf of our enterprise customers as a data processor — that processing is governed by our Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
