# SAFFRON DESIGNS — CLIENT EMAIL SEQUENCE

**Purpose:** Standard email templates for every stage of the client lifecycle.
**Tone:** Professional, warm, clear. Not robotic — customize each before sending.

---

## 1. INITIAL INQUIRY RESPONSE (same day)

> **Subject:** Thanks for reaching out — [Project Type] inquiry
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Thanks for reaching out! I'd love to learn more about your [website / project].
>
> To make sure I understand what you need, could you tell me a bit more about:
> • What kind of website you're looking for (new site, redesign, landing page)?
> • What your business does (a quick summary is fine)?
> • Do you have a timeline and budget in mind?
>
> Once I have a clearer picture, I can put together a proposal tailored to your needs.
>
> In the meantime, feel free to browse my portfolio at saffrondesigns.io.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]
