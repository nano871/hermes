## 1. PURPOSE
The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship (the **"Purpose"**). In connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose Confidential Information to the other.

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
**"Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the **"Disclosing Party"**) to the other (the **"Receiving Party"**), whether orally, in writing, or by any other medium, that:
(a) is marked or identified as confidential at the time of disclosure; or
(b) a reasonable person would understand to be confidential under the circumstances.

Confidential Information includes but is not limited to: business plans, financial information, technical data, software, algorithms, trade secrets, client lists, pricing, product roadmaps, and the existence and terms of this Agreement.

## 3. EXCLUSIONS
Confidential Information does not include information that:
(a) is or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) was rightfully in the Receiving Party's possession before disclosure;
(c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information;
(d) is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality obligations.

## 4. OBLIGATIONS
The Receiving Party shall:
(a) maintain Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party except its officers, employees, and advisors who need to know and are bound by equivalent obligations;
(c) use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(d) protect Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

## 5. REQUIRED DISCLOSURES
If the Receiving Party is compelled by law, regulation, or court order to disclose Confidential Information, it shall promptly notify the Disclosing Party and cooperate in seeking a protective order. Disclosure shall be limited to the minimum extent required.

## 6. TERM
This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **two (2) years**. Confidentiality obligations survive for **five (5) years** from disclosure, or indefinitely for trade secrets.

## 7. RETURN OF MATERIALS
Upon the Disclosing Party's request, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction.

## 8. NO RIGHTS GRANTED
Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any intellectual property rights or license. Neither Party is obligated to proceed with any business relationship.

## 9. NO WARRANTY
All Confidential Information is provided "AS IS" without warranty. Neither Party shall be liable for use of Confidential Information except as expressly set forth in this Agreement.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Minnesota. Exclusive venue: Hennepin County, MN.
