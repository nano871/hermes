# SAFFRON DESIGNS — SUBCONTRACTOR ONBOARDING CHECKLIST

**Purpose:** Legal and operational steps when bringing on a freelancer, developer, or agency partner.
**Use before:** Any subcontractor touches a client project.

---

## LEGAL CHECKLIST

| # | Item | Status | Notes |
|---|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | **Signed Independent Contractor Agreement** | ☐ | Use `saffron-contractor-agreement.md`. Must include: IP assignment, confidentiality, IC classification. |
| 2 | **IP assignment confirmed** | ☐ | Agreement must clearly state all work product belongs to Saffron (or the client). No "I retain rights to reuse code." |
| 3 | **AI disclosure obtained** | ☐ | Sub must disclose if they use AI tools. If yes: AI-generated content must be reviewed before delivery. Saffron's AI disclaimers flow down to client. |
| 4 | **Confidentiality agreement** | ☐ | Sub must be bound by confidentiality covering client data, business information, project details. |
| 5 | **Non-solicitation (optional)** | ☐ | Sub cannot poach Saffron's clients or employees for [12] months after engagement. |
| 6 | **Insurance verified** | ☐ | Sub must have GL insurance ($1M minimum). Request certificate of insurance. |
| 7 | **W-9 collected** | ☐ | Required for 1099-NEC at year-end if payments exceed $600. |
| 8 | **Payment terms confirmed** | ☐ | Rate, invoicing schedule, net terms, late payment penalty. |
| 9 | **Scope of work defined** | ☐ | Written SOW or statement of deliverables. No out-of-scope work without approval. |

## OPERATIONAL CHECKLIST

| # | Item | Status | Notes |
|---|------|--------|-------|
| 10 | **Project access granted** | ☐ | CMS, staging site, design files, brand guidelines |
| 11 | **Communication channel established** | ☐ | Email, Slack, or project management tool |
| 12 | **Client context shared** | ☐ | Project brief, client background, brand guidelines, goals |
| 13 | **Quality standards shared** | ☐ | Code standards, design conventions, browser support, performance targets |
| 14 | **Deadlines confirmed** | ☐ | Sub knows the timeline and their deliverables |
| 15 | **Review process defined** | ☐ | Sub delivers to Saffron → Saffron reviews → Saffron delivers to client |
| 16 | **Backup/continuity confirmed** | ☐ | Sub's files accessible to Saffron (not only on sub's machine) |

## RED FLAGS — DO NOT ENGAGE

| Red Flag | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Sub refuses to sign IC agreement | You have no IP protection. Sub could claim ownership of client work. |
| Sub says "I own my code" | Client paid for the work. Saffron needs to deliver full IP to client. |
| Sub uses only AI-generated code and won't review it | You're warranting the code to the client. AI code can have security vulnerabilities. |
| Sub has no insurance | If sub causes a data breach or site outage, you're liable. |
| Sub works without a written scope | Guaranteed scope creep and disputes. |
